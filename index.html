Bonjour, Hola, & Hello

I'm Karla Ramos

Just a small-town Mexican/American girl with a passion for adventure. I was born and raised in the border-city of El Paso, TX. Growing up I had the best of both worlds. My household made me the proud Mexican I am, while school and friends made me the patriotic American girl. I enjoy learning new languages, visiting new places, and exploring the unkown.

Sports

I love being active and staying fit. Sports and excercise are a way for me to step outside the hustle-and-bustle of everyday life, its my form of meditation. I grew up as a dancer and a gymnast but transitioned to tennis by the age of eleven. I played competitively for eight years but now it's for fun! I also enjoy watching sports during my free time.

Gastronomy

I am an avid foodie! I love to try food from all over the world. It is a way for me to dive into another culture and learn more about it. I enjoy all types of cuisines but Italian is my favorite. From a young age my mother and aunts instilled a love for being in the kitchen and getting creative.

Music

There is something spectacular about the human connection to music. It is a way to express oneself and connect with the artist. Music is shared by everyone, it brings people together. I enjoy music from every decade and genre. Some of my top artists inlcude David Bowie, The Strokes, and Kings of Leons.
